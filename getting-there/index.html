Getting There

Casa Tilo does not have an official postal address, but you can find it here on Google Maps.

If you’re coming from the city, take the R1 train to Arenys de Mar. If you’re coming from the airport, there’s a direct train to Sant Celoni (R2N). If you give us advance notice we can usually pick you up from the train station.

If you need a taxi, we recommend the app Free Now.
