Getting There

Casa Tilo does not have an official postal address, but you can find it here on Google Maps.

The two nearest train stations are at Sant Celoni to the north and Arenys de Mar to the south. If you’re coming from the airport, there’s a direct train to Sant Celoni (R2N). If you’re coming from the city, take the R1 to Arenys de Mar. If you give us advance notice we can usually pick you up from the train station.

If you need a taxi, we recommend the app Free Now.
